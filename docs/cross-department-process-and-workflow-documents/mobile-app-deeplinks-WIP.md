## Mobile App Deeplinks

The Musora, Drumeo, and Pianote apps use universal deeplinks, meaning that the app's deeplinks share the same URL as the web.<br/><br/>
This means that if the user's device has one of our apps installed, and they click a link that is one of our platform URLs, their phone should automatically attempt to open the mobile app. We then look at that URL and direct the app to the correct page. If we do not support that URL (it is not in the list below), we open the link in their default browser instead.<br/><br/>
